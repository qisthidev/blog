When your Google AdSense payment reaches the threshold, you may receive a notification that your **Tax Residency Certificate** (Surat Keterangan Domisili Pajak) has expired. To continue receiving payments without interruption, you must upload a new certificate—something Indonesian creators are now required to obtain through **CoreTAX**, the updated platform that replaced DJP Online.

This guide walks you through every step, based entirely on the [video tutorial](https://youtu.be/VPl8aJkXDWE) transcript.

## **Why You Need to Renew Your AdSense Tax Residency Certificate**

Google AdSense periodically requires proof of your tax residency status. If the certificate expires—such as on **January 1, 2025** in the example from the transcript—you must submit a new one to continue receiving payments and avoid withholding issues.

Previously, Indonesian taxpayers could generate the certificate via **DJP Online → KSWP**, but this service has been discontinued. All requests are now done through **CoreTAX**.

### **Step 1: Log In to the CoreTAX System**

You will need:

* Your registered **NIK**
* Your **old password** (you will be prompted to change it)

Once logged in, you will enter your CoreTAX dashboard.

### **Step 2: Request the Electronic Certificate Authorization Code**

Inside CoreTAX:

1. Go to **Portal Saya**
2. Select **Kode Otorisasi DJP**
3. Enter your CoreTAX password
4. Click **Save**

This code is required before you can digitally sign documents later.

### **Step 3: Create an Administrative Service Request**

Navigate to:

**Layanan Wajib Pajak → Layanan Administrasi → Buat Permohonan Layanan**

Choose:

* Document type **AS03 – Surat Keterangan Domisili**
* **Surat Keterangan Domisili Subjek Pajak Dalam Negeri**

Click **Save**.

### **Step 4: Fill In the Case Workflow (Alur Kasus)**

This section may load slowly—be patient.

#### **Important fields to complete:**

* **Start Month:** Choose when your certificate should begin (e.g., March)
* **End Month:** Must be in the same year (e.g., December 2025)
* **Transaction Counterparty:**

```txt
Google (Google Asia Pacific Pte. Ltd.)
```

* **Country of Counterparty:**

```txt
Singapore
```

* **TIN Number:**

```txt
20081794R
```

* **Transaction Description:** *Google AdSense Payment*

All of this is directly mentioned in the transcript.

### **Step 5: Generate the PDF Document**

Click **Create PDF**, then fill out:

* Title: *Permohonan Surat Keterangan Domisili SPDNAS*
* Document Type: **Original**
* Classification: **Confidential**
* Document Description: *Google AdSense Payment*
* Tag: **Baru**
* Tax Type: **PPh Pasal 21**
* Leave the Tax Year and Tax Month blank

Save the document.

### **Step 6: Sign the Document Digitally**

Click **Sign / Tanda Tangani**, then **Save**.

This final step validates the submission.

## **How Long Does the Certificate Take?**

The transcript explains that generating the certificate takes **approximately 24 hours**.

After that, you can check:

> **Dokumen Saya → Refresh**

Once issued, the certificate will appear with a date (example: *21 March 2025*).

Download the file.

## **Upload the Tax Residency Certificate to Google AdSense**

In your AdSense account:

1. Open the tax section
2. Upload the downloaded certificate
3. Click **Submit**

Verification typically completes much faster than the stated 7 working days.
